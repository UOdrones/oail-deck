The OPERRA Story
How a shared planning platform eliminates bottlenecks, streamlines temporary setups, and accelerates field productivity.
________________


1. What OPERRA really does
Instead of operators driving from site to site, Operra is the coordination hub where multiple service companies come together to plan temporary setups. Whether it’s for drilling, completions, or production, everyone collaborates in one virtual space to create the most efficient and productive plan. It’s all about reducing on-site guesswork and making sure each temporary service is set up right the first time.
________________


2. The Real Cost of Doing It the Old Way
In the old approach, each service company might plan in isolation, leading to overlapping efforts, scheduling conflicts, and higher costs. Without planning software, one service company may unknowingly encroach on another’s space, causing delays, rework, and lower efficiency. And without a shared platform, miscommunications can slow equipment delivery or disrupt timelines. Operra solves this by ensuring everyone is aligned from the start, streamlining the entire process and eliminating hidden costs.
________________


3. Why Early Coordination Matters
In the traditional model, any misalignment in planning might not be noticed until teams are already on site, causing delays or complications. With Operra, early, centralized coordination means potential issues are identified and resolved before anyone sets foot on site. This gives you a comprehensive overview that lets you identify bottlenecks and correct them early, minimizing downtime and keeping costs predictable. Early alignment prevents downstream problems, long before they become expensive delays on location.
________________


4. What OPERRA Is and How It Works
Operra is the collaborative planning hub for temporary oilfield setups. It brings together all the service companies involved (drilling, completions, production) and lets them coordinate in a single platform. Everyone can input their requirements, see the whole picture, and align schedules and resources. Operra organizes these inputs into a shared plan that reduces overlap and maximizes efficiency.

5. How OPERRA Changes the Cost Structure
By centralizing planning and coordination, Operra transforms how temporary setups are managed. With a complete overview, you can identify and correct bottlenecks before they become issues, reducing unexpected costs and making the whole operation more efficient and predictable.
By preventing conflicts before they occur, Operra reduces wasted time and creates a smoother execution path for all service providers involved.
